Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a story about hope and friendship. It follows the life of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover. Over the years, he befriends fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding and finds ways to survive the harsh realities of prison life.
